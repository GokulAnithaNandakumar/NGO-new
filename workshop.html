Workshop: Humanity

In the transformative workshop, "Humanity Generate One," participants embark on a journey of exploration, collaboration, and innovation aimed at collectively shaping a harmonious and sustainable future. Over the course of this interactive session, attendees delve into the intersection of technology, ethics, and human potential, seeking to harness the power of unity and creativity for the betterment of our shared existence. Through engaging discussions, hands-on activities, and thought-provoking exercises, the workshop aims to inspire a shared vision where humanity collaboratively generates solutions to pressing challenges, fostering a sense of collective responsibility and empowerment. Join us as we explore the boundless possibilities that arise when diverse minds converge with the common goal of creating a more compassionate, inclusive, and resilient world for generations to come. Panelists:

    • Alice M. Harmony is a humanitarian analyst with 32 years of impactful experience in philanthropy. Appointed as the visionary leader of CompassionateCommunities in 2003, she has dedicated her career to covering diverse topics such as global well-being, sustainable development, and social innovation. Alice's contributions extend beyond borders, having shared stories from Asia and South America with major publications like The Global Harmony Gazette and Philanthropy Today. She served as the guiding force behind the Harmony Hub, was a key player in founding Ethical Endeavors, and early in her career, played a vital role in a team recognized with the Humanitarian Spirit Award. Passionate about creating positive change, Alice finds solace in nature, exploring different cultures, and cherishing moments with her global family.
